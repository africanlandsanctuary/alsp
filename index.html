African Sanctuary Land Project

The African Sanctuary Land Project (ASL), established by Abuburo Kosua, is a way and means to help facilitate the Homecoming of our African Family Members from the African Diaspora.

The Village of Amoanda in Central Region, Ghana, has opened up an area of land that is now dedicated and demarcated as African Sanctuary Land. It is exclusively a land for our Brothers and Sisters to build and develop into their homes!

The African Sanctuary Land Project is currently accepting applications on a rolling basis.
